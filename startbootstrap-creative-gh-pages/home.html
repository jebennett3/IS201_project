Health

Physical

When our bodies are healthy and taken care of, we can take care of other aspects of our health.

Emotional

We all need to take a little bit of time for mindfulness and good music.

Social

The relationships in our lives have strong influence on other areas of our lives. When we have healthy social lives, we often have greater health in other aspects of our lives.

Spiritual

When we have a spiritual aspect and a sense of who God is in our lives, we make better decisions and have higher mental health.
